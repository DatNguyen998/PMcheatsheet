# 📋 Project Management Cheat Sheet (PMcheatsheet)

Welcome to this repository. This is a personal, quiet corner where I am gathering, organizing, and simplifying Project Management (PM) frameworks, methodologies, and templates to help guide my daily learning journey.

---

### 👤 About the Author
I am **Kent**. As a Business Analyst, I often find myself standing right at the bridge between business needs and technical teams. I am naturally a bit shy and prefer to listen and observe, but I have a deep desire to learn how to keep projects running smoothly, transparently, and kindly for everyone involved. 

---

### 🌱 Purpose of This Space
The ultimate goal of this repository is **learning and personal growth**. 
Stepping into the world of project coordination can feel overwhelming with all its terminology and moving parts. I created this cheat sheet to:
*   **Deconstruct Frameworks:** Break down complex Agile, Scrum, and traditional project management concepts into simple, digestible notes.
*   **Improve Daily Work:** Create quick-reference guides that I can use in my daily life to keep tasks organized and track milestones effectively.
*   **Bridge Roles:** Better understand the perspective of Product Managers and Project Managers so I can be a more empathetic and supportive Business Analyst.

---

### 🗂️ What Is Inside
This collection is built one small note at a time and covers:
*   **Agile & Scrum Quick-Refs:** Summaries on sprint planning, user story grooming, and estimation techniques.
*   **Documentation Templates:** Simple, clean frameworks for PRDs (Product Requirement Documents), BRDs (Business Requirement Documents), and meeting minutes.
*   **Prioritization Frameworks:** Notes on how to weigh features and tasks objectively (using frameworks like MoSCoW, RICE, or Kano models).
*   **KPIs & Metrics:** A gentle guide to tracking product health, velocity, and user satisfaction.

---

### 🛠️ How I Use This
*   **Markdown:** For structuring everything beautifully and cleanly so it is easy to read at a single glance.
*   **Daily Application:** Treating this repository as a live document that I update every time I learn a new strategy or discover a better workflow pattern.
